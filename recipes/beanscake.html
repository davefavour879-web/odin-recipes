BEANS CAKE RECIPES

if you're craving something cripsy,golden,and packed with rish plant based flavour ,then this beanscake recipe popularly known as akarain nigeria is your next kitchen adventure. this beloved west african street food is made from peeled black eyed peas(or brown beans), blended with spices,and deep fried to perfection.weather served on a lazy saturday morning or enjoyed on the go,akara remains one of nigeria's most dishes.

But beyound being a nostalgic breakfast favourite,beancake holds nutitional and cultural value that makes it standout.from its fiber rich based to the savory pepper and onion blend,it satisfies your teste buds and nourishes your body.
